About me

[2017-2020]

I have done an engineering school at ENSEEIHT (Toulouse, France) for 3 years. During this time I effected a 2 months internship in the Reva Team at IRIT.

[2020-2021]

I finished my school by doing a 6 months internship at FittingBox (industry) and then continued to full-time work at this place for a year.

[2021-2024]

I started a dual degree PhD between the Image section at DIKU (Copenhagen, Denmark) and the REVA team at IRIT (Toulouse, France) on "3D Reconstruction with Refraction". I am also Teacher Assistant (TA) in the course "Numerical Optimisation" (for master students).
